Triangulation

Triangulation is a very powerful way to track your location.

A computer or a mobile device tracks its location automatically

by continuously sending signals to the Wifi hub or cell towers.

You know you can also do this to track other people?

Same idea.

In social apps like Wechat

or Momo

and thousands of apps out there,

there are features such as "People Nearby" or "Look around" etc.

Usually the exact location appeared on those social apps are hidden,
