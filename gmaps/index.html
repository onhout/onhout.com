Triangulation

Triangulation can be a very powerful way to track your location. In normal Wifi tracking situation, a computer determines it's location automatically by continuously sending signals to the Wifi hub to ask the distance between the computer by how strong or weak the current signal is. You know you can also do this to track other people? Same idea. In social apps like Wechat, or Momo, and thousands of apps out there, there are features such as "People Nearby" or "Look around" etc. Usually the exact location of the people that appeared on those social apps are hidden, but they do give off approximate location.
